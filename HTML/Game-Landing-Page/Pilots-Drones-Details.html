Pilots and Drones

Pilot

pilots are special characters with unique skills that enhance vehicles, categorized as Common (basic bonuses) and Legendary (powerful, vehicle-specific abilities that level up significantly)

Drones

Drones are special companions that link to your vehicle and provide both passive and active effects. They are designed to enhance your combat performance, adapt to your playstyle, and add an entirely new layer of strategy to Massive Warfare
